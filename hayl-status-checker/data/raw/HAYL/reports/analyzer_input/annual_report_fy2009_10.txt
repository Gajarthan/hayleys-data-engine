Hayleys PLC - Annual Report 2009/10
Founded in 1878 as Chas P. Hayley & Company, Hayleys is today one of the largest
Sri Lankan multinationals. It is a publicly quoted company with a portfolio of globally
competitive core businesses in Global Markets & Manufacturing, Agriculture &
Agri Business, Transportation & Infrastructure and Consumer Products & Leisure.
In the 58 years since its incorporation as Hayleys, it has a record of 26 scrip issues
and 4 modestly-priced rights issues, together with dividend payouts averaging 25% in
each of these years.
Hayleys today accounts for 2.45% of Sri Lanka’s export income and carries a AA-(lka)
credit rating by Fitch Ratings.
